About Me

I believe the world is an infinitely interesting place and I wish I could see and experience all of it. New experiences that foster a deeper understanding of how things work are what drives me. Currently, I'm working to find my future in the field of web development.


I have worked in research, aviation manufacturing, the marijuana industry, customer service, and the fine arts and entertainment industries. I have made it to Denver via Albuquerque and Washington D.C. and currently share an apartment with my cat and partner.


I enjoy getting lost in hobbies and projects whether it be painting, crocheting, cooking, chemistry, gardening, dance or writing code. I love nature, art, science, math, festivals and friends and am always down for a beer, a board game, or a good campfire!
